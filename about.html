Who We Are

Heritage rooted in Sabi Gold Mine

From the first pegged claims in 1890 to the present-day ANMACK MINING partnership, Sabi Gold Mine has continuously adapted, retooled, and invested in Zimbabwe’s mining future.

Resilience through the decades

The mine’s production first surged in 1909. After joining the Zimbabwe Mining Development Corporation portfolio, Sabi Gold experienced both celebrated milestones and demanding restarts, culminating in its 2017 relaunch under judicial management.

In 2021, a strategic alliance with Chandiwana Mining Corporation ushered in the ANMACK MINING (Pvt) Ltd era, combining world-class technical know-how, stringent governance, and capital reinvestment to revitalise the Midlands Province operation.

Today’s footprint

  • Zvishavane Rutenga Road · Midlands Province, Zimbabwe
  • Integrated underground & surface operations
  • Gold extraction, processing, and beneficiation
  • Local procurement and community partnerships

Our Mission

Maximise stakeholder value through safe exploitation of gold reserves in a cost-effective, profitable, and sustainable manner while equitably rewarding employees and partners.

Our Vision

Become one of the most consistent gold producers in Zimbabwe and the region.

Milestones that shaped us

  1. 1890

    Claims first pegged in the Sabi region, setting the foundation for the mine.

  2. 1909

    Recorded production begins, positioning Sabi as a key gold contributor.

  3. 2014

    Operations pause to restructure costs and settle accumulated debt.

  4. 2017

    Mine reopens under judicial management with renewed operational discipline.

  5. 2021

    ANMACK MINING partnership launches, propelling modernisation and growth.
