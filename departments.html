Department Overview

The HR team attracts, develops, and retains top mining talent. We enable positive, performance-driven engagements throughout the employee lifecycle: onboarding, growth, wellness, and offboarding.

Services include industrial relations, human capital development, records management, clinic and guesthouse operations, and an early childhood development centre plus CSR programmes for surrounding communities.

Key Functions

  • Talent acquisition and recruitment
  • Employee onboarding & orientation
  • Performance management frameworks
  • Training & professional development
  • Compensation & benefits administration
  • Employee relations & engagement
  • Health, safety & wellness services
  • Corporate social responsibility initiatives

Accounting Department

Financial guardianship across every production cycle.

Department Overview

Accounting manages financial resources, produces accurate reporting, and ensures compliance with regulatory bodies. Insightful analytics support strategic decision-making and long-term shareholder value.

The department includes the creditors office, cash office, time office, and accountants who maintain rigorous internal controls and cost tracking.

Key Functions

  • Financial reporting & analysis
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Cost control & management
  • Accounts payable & receivable
  • Payroll processing
  • Tax planning & compliance
  • Internal controls & auditing
  • Capital expenditure management
